Dashboards

In Cyclotron, Dashboards consist of one or more Pages, each of which has one or more Widgets. A Dashboard has several top-level properties, many of which are inherited by Pages and Widgets. Setting a property at the Dashboard-level can be more efficient than setting it directly on every Widget. Inherited properties can be overridden by the Page and/or Widget, as well.

In the Dashboard Editor, these properties are found under the Details tab, although pages, dataSources, scripts, etc. are located in their own tab in the Editor.
